## What I Understood

The junior researcher implemented a Genetic Algorithm (GA) with Topology Crossover for the Santa 2025 Christmas tree packing problem. The hypothesis was that by exchanging tree ARRANGEMENTS between parent solutions (rather than just optimizing positions), the GA could explore a fundamentally different search space than SA/gradient methods. Two approaches were tried: (1) GA with uniform/region crossover, swap mutation, and group rotation mutation, and (2) Aggressive GA with greedy construction from scratch. Both approaches found NO improvements across all 200 N values.

This is the 13th experiment in a series that has achieved only 0.000521 total improvement (0.0007%) from the baseline. The current score is 70.659437, and the target is 68.919154 (a gap of 1.74 points, or 2.5%).
