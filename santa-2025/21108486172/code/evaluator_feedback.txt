The overlapping configs (67.727) represent a fundamentally better packing structure. The key insight is that they were optimized WITHOUT collision constraints and found a different basin of attraction.

### Proposed Approach: Gradual Constraint Tightening

1. **Start from the overlapping configuration** for each N value
2. **Identify the overlapping pairs** and their overlap areas
3. **Gradually tighten constraints** by:
   - Moving overlapping trees apart by small amounts
   - Rotating trees to reduce overlap
   - Accepting moves that reduce total overlap area
4. **Use a penalty function** that balances:
   - Score (bounding box size)
   - Overlap penalty (weighted by overlap area)
5. **Gradually increase the overlap penalty** until overlaps are eliminated

### Why This Might Work

1. The overlapping configs are in a BETTER basin - we want to stay in that basin while eliminating overlaps
2. Small overlaps might be fixable with small adjustments
3. The penalty function approach allows the optimizer to trade off score vs. validity
