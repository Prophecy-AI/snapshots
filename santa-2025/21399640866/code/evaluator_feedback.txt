## What I Understood

The junior researcher implemented a centrosymmetric placement approach per my previous recommendation. The hypothesis was that placing trees in symmetric pairs around a center point (tree i at (x, y, θ) and tree i+N/2 at (-x, -y, θ+180°)) could exploit the tree's bilateral symmetry to achieve better packings. Three patterns were tested: ring, spiral, and grid, on 12 even N values (4-50). The result: ALL patterns produced configurations WORSE than baseline - none could beat the baseline scores.
