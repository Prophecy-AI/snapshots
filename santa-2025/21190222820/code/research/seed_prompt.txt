### 2. CRYSTALLINE PACKING WITH PLANE-GROUP SYMMETRIES
Research shows 17 plane-group symmetries can achieve optimal packings:
- p1: No symmetry (most flexible)
- p2: 180° rotation only
- p4: 90° rotation (square lattice)
- p6: 60° rotation (hexagonal lattice)
- cm, pm, pg: Mirror and glide symmetries

**Implementation:**

### 4. MINKOWSKI SUM-BASED PLACEMENT
Use Minkowski sums to compute exact no-overlap regions:
- For each placed tree, compute forbidden region for next tree
- Place next tree at boundary of forbidden region
- This guarantees tight packing
